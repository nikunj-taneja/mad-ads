# Customer research

Read `templates/customer-research.md`, `variables/personas.md`, and all operator-provided sources. Preserve exact quotes and source IDs. Remove unnecessary personal identifiers.

## Modes

- `ingest`: append normalized evidence to a private registry supplied by the operator.
- `mine`: cluster repeated situations, desired outcomes, objections, and language.

Do not turn one vivid quote into a segment. State sample size and source bias. Never fabricate demographic traits.

Write proposals to `creative-strategy/persona-update-YYMMDD.md` with evidence, counter-evidence, uncertainty, and the exact suggested variable change. Do not edit `variables/personas.md` until the operator approves.
