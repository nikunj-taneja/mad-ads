# Creative formats

| Code | Format | Required assets | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| FND | Founder story | Founder footage or approved avatar | Use only verified founder facts |
| UGC | Customer-style demonstration | Creator, product, environment | Do not present actors as real customers |
| POD | Product demonstration | Product footage | Show the mechanism or use clearly |
| STA | Static image | Product image, headline | One message per execution |
| CAR | Carousel | 3 to 10 coordinated cards | Each card must advance the argument |
